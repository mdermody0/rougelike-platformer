about the game

The game that I have made is as the title says, it's a roguelike platformer. If you're unfamiliar with what a roguelike is.
Getting its name from the 1980 game Rogue, a roguelike is a type of game where upon death whatever progress you made is reset as you try to complete your objective.
As this is a platformer, kind of like Super Mario, your objective is to make it to the end of the level.
